The Gruffalo
🐭 🦊 🦉 🐍 🧌
Inspired by Julia Donaldson • A clever rhyming tale!
A mouse took a walk through the
deep dark wood.
A fox saw the mouse
and the mouse looked good.
🐭 🌲🌳🌲
🦊
"Where are you going, little mouse?
Come and have lunch at my house!"
🐭
"I'm having lunch with a Gruffalo!"
A Gruffalo?
🦷 Terrible tusks
🦶 Terrible claws
👁️ Orange eyes
🎯 A wart on his nose!
"The Gruffalo's favourite food
is roasted fox!"

"Oh!" said the fox,
and away he ran! 💨
🦊💨   😏🐭
"There's no such thing
as a Gruffalo!"
thought the mouse with a smile.
🦉
"Where are you going, little mouse?
Come and have tea at my house!"
🐭
"I'm having tea with a Gruffalo!"
A Gruffalo?
🪲 Purple prickles on his back
🦮 A poisonous wart
👁️ Orange eyes
🦷 Terrible teeth!
"The Gruffalo's favourite food
is owl ice cream!"

"Oh!" said the owl,
and away he flew! 💨
🦉💨   😏🐭
🐍
"Where are you going, little mouse?
Come for a feast at my logpile house!"
🐭
"I'm having a feast with a Gruffalo!"
A Gruffalo?
🎯 Knobbly knees
👄 Turned-out toes
🟠 Orange eyes
🪱 Scrambled snake!
"The Gruffalo's favourite food
is scrambled snake!"

"Oh!" said the snake,
and away he slithered! 💨
🐍💨   😄🐭
"Silly old fox, owl and snake!"
chuckled the mouse.
"There's no such thing as a Gruffalo!"
But who is this creature
with terrible claws
and terrible tusks
and terrible jaws?
🧌
"Oh help! Oh no!
IT IS THE GRUFFALO!"
🧌
"My favourite food!"
said the Gruffalo.
"Roasted mouse on toast!"
😤🐭
"Roasted me?
That's funny!
I am the scariest
creature in this wood!"
"Walk behind me!" said the mouse.
They walked through the deep dark wood...

🐍 Snake saw them — and slithered away!
🦉 Owl saw them — and flew away!
🦊 Fox saw them — and ran away!
🐭➡️🧌
🧌😱
The Gruffalo was terrified!

All the animals ran from
the mouse and him!

"You really ARE the scariest
creature in this wood!"
And away the Gruffalo ran! 💨
🐭
The mouse found a nut
and the nut was good.

The best nut ever!
In the deep dark wood. 🌰
🌰 😄 🌲
🐭
The End!
Being clever is better than being big! 🧠✨
