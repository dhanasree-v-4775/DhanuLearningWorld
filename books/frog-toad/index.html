Story 1
🌸 Spring
🐊🛌
Toad was in bed, fast asleep.
He had been sleeping all winter long.
Snore… snore… snore… 💤
🐸
Frog
Toad! Wake up!
It is spring!
🌸☀️🌱
🐊
Toad
Blah!
Go away!
I am sleeping! 💤
Frog ran to Toad's calendar
on the wall.
He tore off all the winter pages —
📅✂️
❄️❌ 🌸✅
"Look, Toad! The page says MAY!
It really IS spring!"
🐊
Toad
Oh my!
You are right!
It IS spring! 🌸
🐸🐊
They ran outside together
into the warm sunshine
to play all day long! ☀️🌿
Story 2
📮 The Letter
🐊😔
Toad sat on his porch, looking sad.

"What is wrong?" asked Frog.

"I am waiting for the mail,
but I never get any letters.
Nobody ever writes to me."
🐊😢
Toad
It is sad to have
no letters. Nobody
thinks about me.
🐸
Frog
I think about you!
Don't worry, Toad.
Wait right here!
Frog ran home and wrote Toad a letter:
Dear Toad,

I am so glad that you
are my best friend.

Your best friend,
Frog 🐸
✉️🐌📮
He gave it to a snail to deliver.
🐸🐊
Frog and Toad waited
and waited for the snail.
They waited all day…
🐌🐌🐌
The snail was
very, very slow.
🐸😴 🐊😴
🐊😁
Toad
A letter!
A letter for ME!
🎉📮❤️
🐸😊
Frog
I am so happy
you are happy,
Toad! 😄
Story 3
🔘 A Lost Button
🐊😟🧥
Toad lost a button from his jacket
while walking with Frog.

"Oh no! My big white button
with four holes — it is GONE!"

They searched everywhere. 🔍
🐸🐊
⚫ 🔵 🟤 ⚪
They found buttons everywhere —
black ones, blue ones, small ones —
but none was Toad's button!

"This is not my button!" cried Toad.
🐊😊🧥
When Toad got home,
he found his button
on the floor! 🔘
🧵🔘🎁
Toad sewed ALL the found buttons
onto his jacket
and gave it to Frog as a gift!
🐸🥹❤️
Story 4
🏊 The Swim
🐊🩳😳
Frog invited Toad to go swimming.

Toad said he would swim,
but he did NOT want anyone
to see him in his bathing suit!

"I look funny in my bathing suit."
🐸🦆🐢🐍
All the animals laughed!
🐊😠
Toad
Now I know why
you wanted me to
swim with you!
So they could laugh! 😤
🐸🤝🐊
The End!
True friends stick together
through every season! 🌸🍂❄️☀️
