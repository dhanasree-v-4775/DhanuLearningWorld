Magic Tree House #1
Dinosaurs Before Dark
🧒👧 📚 ⏰ 🦖 🌟
Inspired by Mary Pope Osborne • A time-travel adventure!
🧒👧
Jack & Annie
Eight-year-old Jack
and his sister Annie
were walking through
the Frog Creek woods
one afternoon...

"Look! Up in that oak tree!" 🌳
🌳🏠
High up in a tall oak tree
was a tree house!

It was filled with books —
hundreds of books! 📚📚📚

Whose tree house was it?
Where did all the books come from?
👧
Annie
Come on, Jack!
Let's climb up! 🪜
🧒📓
Jack
Wait! We should
find out whose
it is first! 🤔
But he climbed up anyway!
📚🔍
Jack found a book about
dinosaurs! 🦕

He opened to a picture of
a flying reptile — a Pteranodon!
📓 Jack's Notes:
Pteranodon = flying reptile
• Not a dinosaur!
• Wingspan: 7 metres!
• No teeth — beak like a bird
🧒📖✨
Jack pointed to the Pteranodon picture.

"I wish I could see a
real Pteranodon," he said.

Suddenly the wind began to blow! 💨

The tree house began to SPIN! 🌪️
🌀
🌀⭐🌀
The tree house spun
faster and faster!

Then — everything stopped.

They had travelled back in time —
65 million years!
⏰ → 🦕
🦕🌿🌴🦖
They looked out of the window.
The Frog Creek woods were GONE!

Instead — a dinosaur world!

Tall fern trees. A wide river.
And in the sky above them…
🦅
A real Pteranodon!
👧
Annie
Hello! I won't
hurt you! 😊
You're beautiful!
🦅
The Pteranodon landed
beside Annie!

Annie gently patted
its golden crest.

"I'm going to call you Henry!"
🧒📓
Jack
I must write
everything down!
This is amazing! ✍️
📓 Jack's field notes:
✅ Pteranodon is real!
✅ Not scary — gentle!
✅ Golden crest on head
✅ Annie is very brave 😄
🦖
ROAAARRR! 💥
A Tyrannosaurus Rex
thundered out of the trees!

BOOM! BOOM! BOOM!
The ground shook with every step! 🌍

"RUN!" yelled Jack. 🏃
🧒👧😱
Jack dropped his backpack!
The T-Rex stopped to sniff it.
That gave them time!
🦅
Henry the Pteranodon
swooped down!

He carried Jack and Annie
safely back to the tree house! 🏠
🧒👧📖
Jack grabbed the book
about Frog Creek woods
and pointed to a picture.

"I wish we could go home!"
🌀
The tree house spun again… ✨
🧒👧
They were back in
Frog Creek woods!

Same day. Same time.
Like nothing happened! ⏰
✨📜✨
Jack noticed something
in the tree house —
a gold medallion with
the letter M.

Who does it belong to?
🪄
Who is M?
The tree house belongs to
someone magical...

Someone with the letter M.

Could it be Morgan le Fay?
A powerful librarian magician!

Find out in the next book! 📚
🌳🏠⭐
The End!
