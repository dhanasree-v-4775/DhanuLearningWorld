On a warm moonlit night,
a tiny egg rested quietly
on a fresh green leaf.
☀️
🐛
On Sunday morning, the warm sun
rose up, and — POP!
Out came a tiny, very hungry caterpillar!
🗓 Monday He nibbled through
one big red apple.
But he was still hungry!
🍎
1
🍐🍐
2
🗓 Tuesday He munched through
two sweet pears.
But he was still hungry!
🗓 Wednesday He chomped through
three purple plums.
But he was still hungry!
🍇🍇🍇
3
🍓🍓
🍓🍓
4
🗓 Thursday He gobbled through
four juicy strawberries.
But he was still hungry!
🗓 Friday He crunched through
five yummy oranges.
But he was still hungry!
🍊🍊🍊
🍊🍊
5
🗓 Saturday — Big Feast! He ate through everything!
🍰 🍦 🥒 🍩 🍭 🎂 🍧 🌮 🍕 🍉
That night he had a tummy ache! 🤢
🍃
The next day, he ate
just one tiny green leaf.
And he felt much better! 😌
🐛
Now he was big and fat.
He built a small house around himself,
called a cocoon.
🫘
He stayed inside for two whole weeks.

What would happen next? 🤔
He pushed… and pushed…
and nibbled through the cocoon…
🦋
He was a beautiful butterfly!
🦋
The End
