My Father's Dragon
🧒 🌴 🐅 🐊 🦁 🦍 🐉
Inspired by Ruth Stiles Gannett • A jungle rescue adventure!
🧒
Elmer Elevator
🐱
One rainy day, Elmer found
a stray old cat.

The cat told him a secret:
"A baby dragon is trapped
on Wild Island!
You must rescue him!" 🐉
Elmer packed his knapsack with
everything he might need!
🍭🍭🍭 🪥🪥🪥 🔍 ✂️ 🎀 🫧 🦴 🍫
🍭 Lollipops – lots of them
🪥 Toothbrushes – 7 of them
🔍 Magnifying glass
✂️ Scissors & ribbons
🫧 Bubble gum
🦴 Rubber bones
Why these things? You'll see! 😏
Elmer sailed across the ocean
and reached Wild Island!
⛵ 🌊 🌴🌿🌴
The jungle was dark and thick.
Wild animals lived everywhere!

"I must find the baby dragon…"
said Elmer bravely. 💪
🐅🐅🐅🐅
6 tigers blocked the path!
"We are going to eat you!"
🧒
"Would you like some lollipops?"
said Elmer.

🍭🍭🍭🍭🍭🍭

The tigers were SO happy,
they let Elmer pass! 🎉
🦏
A rhinoceros blocked the path!
"You cannot pass!"
🧒🔍
Elmer pulled out his
magnifying glass!

"Look at that tiny bug
on your horn!"

The rhino looked and looked
and Elmer ran past! 🏃
🦁🦁🦁
Lions blocked the path!
"We will eat you for lunch!"
🧒✂️🎀
"Would you like a haircut
and pretty ribbons?"

✂️🎀✂️🎀✂️🎀

The lions LOVED their new look
and let Elmer pass! 😄
🦍🦍🦍🦍🦍
Gorillas blocked the path!
"We won't let you through!"
🧒🫧
"Want some bubble gum?"

🫧🫧🫧🫧🫧

The gorillas chewed and blew
enormous bubbles!
They forgot all about Elmer! 😂
🐊🐊🐊🐊🐊🐊🐊
7 crocodiles in the river!
"We'll snap you up!"
🧒🪥
"Would you like a toothbrush
each?"

🪥🪥🪥🪥🪥🪥🪥

The crocodiles brushed their teeth
and Elmer used them as
stepping stones to cross! 🦶
Finally — Elmer found the
baby dragon!
He was tied up with a rope. 😢
🐉⛓️
"Don't worry!" said Elmer.
"I will set you free!"

He used his scissors to cut
the rope — SNIP! SNIP! SNIP! ✂️
🐉
The dragon is FREE!
🧒😁
The dragon was SO happy!

He nuzzled Elmer with his
golden-yellow wings. 💛

"Let's fly home!" said Elmer.
🐉
🧒 ↑ ☁️ 🌈 ☁️
Together they soared up
into the bright blue sky!

Over the ocean, over the clouds,
all the way home! 🏠❤️
🐅🦏🦁🦍🐊
Back on Wild Island,
all the animals looked up.

"The dragon is gone!"

They were very surprised.

But a small boy with a
knapsack full of treats
had outsmarted them all! 😏
💡
What did Elmer teach us?

🧠 Being clever beats being strong

❤️ Kindness can win over anyone

🎒 Always be prepared!

🐉 Friendship is worth any adventure
🐉
The End!
Bravery + cleverness = any adventure! 🌟
