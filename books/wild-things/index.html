🌙 🌊 🏝️ 👑
Inspired by Maurice Sendak • A wild adventure!
🐺
Max
The night Max wore his wolf suit,
he made all kinds of mischief!

He chased the dog! 🐕
He hammered nails! 🔨
He made a terrible mess! 😈
😤
His mother called him
"WILD THING!"

And Max said
"I'LL EAT YOU UP!"

So he was sent to bed
without his supper. 🍽️
That very night, in Max's room,
a forest began to grow...
🌱 🌿 🌳 🌲 🌳
The walls became the world all around!
The ceiling became the sky!
The room had disappeared...
⛵
A private boat waited for Max.

He sailed away...
through night and day 🌙☀️
in and out of weeks
and almost over a year...
🌊🌊🌊
👹 🦁 🐊 🦇 👺
He arrived at the place
where the Wild Things are!

They roared their terrible roars!
🦷 They gnashed their terrible teeth!
👀 They rolled their terrible eyes!
🦶 They showed their terrible claws!
🐺
👑
But Max looked into their eyes
and did not blink!

"BE STILL!" said Max.

And they were still.

Max tamed the Wild Things!
👑
"Let the wild rumpus start!"
👹🕺👺💃🦁🎉
Max became the King
of all Wild Things!
They danced and roared
all through the night!
😔
But then Max wanted to be
where someone loved him most of all.

From far away he could smell
something good to eat...

He wanted to go home. 🏠
👹👺🦁
"Oh please don't go!
We'll eat you up
we love you so!"
🐺
"No," said Max.

He stepped into his
private boat and
waved goodbye. 👋
⛵
He sailed back over a year
and in and out of weeks
and through a day...

🌊 🌙 ☀️ 🌊
🏠
He arrived back in his own room!

The forest was gone.
His room was his room.

And his supper was waiting for him...
Still hot! 🍽️🥛
👑
The End
Home is where the people
who love you are! ❤️
